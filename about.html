INTRODUCTION

Hi, my names Jack. I'm from Ontario, and at the time of writing this I'm 16 years old(july 2021). As far back I can remember, I've always been tinkering with things and trying to figure out what makes them work. This fueled my interest in Computer Science with a passion to understand how the world opperates through computers and code. Over the past five years or so, I've observed my interests and skills refine towards analytical and rational ideas like with math, computers and programming. This idea further cements and incapsulates my natural tendance towards interests in rational and cogent ideas and thinking which serves as the source of my attraction to Computer Science.

EXPERIENCE

Heres a top 10 list of my relavent experiences, in no particular order

  • Over a decade of familiarity and experience with Windows
  • Fluent with MacOS
  • Intermediate level of knowledge in C++, Java and Python
  • A comfortable and extensive amount of experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience in creating and completing large scale projects concerning both hardware and software
  • Lead programmer in a successful High-School robotics club over two years, including the use of the OpenCV library
  • A fair amount of experience using Unity and C# to create games and applications
  • Familiar experience with the Python Requests library for HTTP requests
  • Years of experience in multiple different IDE's, such as Visual Studio, VScode, notepad++, Adobe Dreamweaver and Adobe Brackets
  • Comfortability and experience with the MacOS terminal and command line interfaces
